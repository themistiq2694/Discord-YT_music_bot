1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Discord YT Music Bot ("the Bot") handles user data. By adding or using the Bot in your Discord server, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

The Bot does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The Bot only processes the minimum information required for functionality:

Discord message content used to execute commands

Voice channel connection data

Basic server metadata provided automatically by Discord

No data is stored outside Discord. No logs, analytics, or tracking systems are used.

3. How Data Is Used

Any data processed by the Bot is used only to:

Execute bot commands

Connect to voice channels

Stream audio content

The Bot does not use data for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with:

Discord API for bot functionality

YouTube Music for audio streaming

These services may have their own privacy policies. Users are encouraged to review them separately.

5. Data Sharing

The Bot does not share any data with third parties.

6. Security

The Bot does not store user data, reducing the risk of unauthorized access. All interactions occur within Discord's secure environment.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended for general use and does not knowingly collect information from children.
